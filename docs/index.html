Questions

Good to know

How do I find last-minute tee times in Perth?

Open the search tool, set today or tomorrow's date and a radius that works for you, and search — every course in range gets checked at once, so you see what's genuinely free right now rather than refreshing one club's site at a time.

Can I search for a big group?

Turn on "Lads, we're on!" mode to find genuine back-to-back tee times with room for four — built for exactly this, so a bigger group can book adjacent slots and play as one pack.
